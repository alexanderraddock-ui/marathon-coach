Race Day Fueling Plan

Coach · CIM Race Plan
For sub-3 you need 60–90g carbs/hour. CIM has aid stations every mile. Plan: Maurten 320 drink in first 5K, then gel at miles 5, 10, 15, 18, 21. Practice this exact protocol on long runs from Week 20 onward — your gut needs training too.
Carb loading: 2 days before, aim for 10g/kg body weight from pasta, rice, bagels. Cut fat and fiber. Race morning: 150g carbs 2.5hrs before start, then sip electrolyte drink to the line.

Brain-Body Activation · Before Every Run

Inspired by Joe Holder's neuromuscular priming approach — these 3 moves take 90 seconds and wake up the neural pathways between your brain and legs before any workout.

1. Single-leg balance with eyes closed
30 sec each side. Forces the nervous system to activate proprioceptive pathways. If you're wobbling a lot, your CNS isn't ready to run fast.
2. Alternating knee drive + opposite arm
20 reps slow, deliberate. Trains the contralateral coordination pattern used in running — right leg drives with left arm. Wakes up the motor cortex.
3. Hip circle + leg swing combo
10 hip circles each direction, then 10 forward/back leg swings each leg. Lubricates the hip joint and signals the glutes to be primary movers rather than the hip flexors.
